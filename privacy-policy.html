Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21 June 2025

Millionaire – Football GOAT ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Millionaire – Football GOAT mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Gameplay & usage data (e.g., scores, levels, session length)

  • Diagnostics (crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Optional account details (email / Google Play Games ID)
  • Advertising identifiers (Google's Advertising ID or Apple’s IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate geolocation (based on IP)

Our Service is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have unintentionally done so, please contact us and we will delete the information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain the Service and player progression
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Analyze performance, detect fraud, and fix bugs
  • Show contextual ads and reward videos (with your consent where required)
  • Personalize your experience and ads (with your consent where required)
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party platforms that may independently process your data:

  • Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics) – App performance monitoring and crash reporting

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review those policies for more information.

4. Data Retention & Security

Gameplay data is retained as long as needed to operate the Service. Crash logs are typically deleted after 90 days. We use encryption during data transmission and access control mechanisms to protect your information.
